Privacy Policy

Last Updated: [DD/MM/YYYY]

At YogSec, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal and business information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you visit our website or use our security services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal information such as name, email address, and contact details
  • Business or website details provided for security assessments
  • Communication data when you contact us via forms or email
  • Technical information such as IP address, browser type, and access logs

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used strictly for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • Providing free website security health checks
  • Performing website security audits and penetration testing
  • Preparing and delivering security assessment reports
  • Responding to inquiries and support requests
  • Improving our services and website experience

3. Security Testing & Ethical Use

All security testing performed by YogSec is conducted with the website owner’s consent and follows responsible disclosure practices. We do not exploit vulnerabilities for malicious purposes, access private user data, or disrupt services.

4. Data Confidentiality

All security findings, reports, and assessment results are treated as strictly confidential. YogSec will never share, sell, or disclose your data or vulnerabilities to third parties without your explicit consent, unless required by law.

5. Data Storage & Protection

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. Access to sensitive information is limited to authorized personnel only.

6. Cookies & Analytics

YogSec may use cookies or analytics tools to understand website usage and improve performance. These tools do not collect sensitive personal data and are used only for analytical purposes.

7. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services for hosting, analytics, or communication. These services are required to handle your data securely and in compliance with applicable privacy standards.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the information we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing where applicable

9. Data Retention

We retain personal and assessment-related data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law.

10. Children’s Privacy

YogSec services are not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
